==> Autoscaling group

Why Auto Scaling?
====================

During events like Big Billion Day Sale, traffic is unpredictable.

You can't manually scale fast enough.

Auto Scaling helps by:
======================

Increasing instances when traffic goes up

Decreasing instances when traffic goes down
